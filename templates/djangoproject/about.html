Welcome to Rate Your Professor

The site that finally gives students a say. RYP is essentially a giant sounding board; so instead of hoping a friend knows how good a lecturer is you can visit this site and see reviews put up by other students whilst adding your own. This is the only way to improve teaching in the Pakistan whilst holding your lecturers to account. Please do take a couple of minutes to add your thoughts and help out your fellow students!
